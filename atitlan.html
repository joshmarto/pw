Atitlan Lake



The Atitlan Lake is considered as one of the most beautiful in the world. Is surronded by Mayan towns which have a lot of culture. On the lake are visibles Mayan people with their handicraft boats flooding in it.

The tour departs at 7 am starting the trip to Atitlan Lake around 3 hours. It arrives to Panajachel, the town at the border of the lake which the boat trips departs on. You will cross the lake and visit the Mayan population of Santiago Atitlan where the guide will explain you their culture and costums.

The price per person in USD is 85.00.
