Tikal



During this tour you will visit the great city of Tikal, the main city on the pre-colonial age.

Our staff pick you up at 4:45 am at your hotel to take you to the airport. After the check-in, the flight will departure at 6 am. Around the 7 am you will be arriving to Flores (which is the airport's name) and our personal will be waiting you with a sign with your name. Then you will be taked to Tikal, this trip takes around 1 hour. Inside of Tikal, the guide will take you trought the jungle to visit the most important monuments and temples. You will have the chance to ride to the top of temple IV which has 230 ft.

The price per person in USD is 350.00.
