Chichicastenango



The Chichicastenango handicraft market is where the recent Mayan populations get together to practice the exchange as their ancesters. The culture, religions, and customs can be clearly visible here. You will have the chance to visit and see the beauty of Atitlan Lake.

The tour starts at 7 am, after to pick you at you hotel, our staff will begin the trip to Chichicastenango village which is at 3 hours from Guatemala City. Then our guide will explain you everthing about the handicraft market which they do on days Thursdays and Sundays

The price per person in USD is 85.00.
