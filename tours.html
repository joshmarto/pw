Tikal



During this tour you will visit the great city of Tikal, the main city on the pre-colonial age.

Our staff pick you up at 4:45 am at your hotel to take you to the airport. After the check-in, the flight will departure at 6 am. Around the 7 am you will be arriving to Flores (which is the airport's name) and our personal will be waiting you with a sign with your name. Then you will be taked to Tikal, this trip takes around 1 hour. Inside of Tikal, the guide will take you trought the jungle to visit the most important monuments and temples. You will have the chance to ride to the top of temple IV which has 230 ft.

The price per person in USD is 350.00.

Chichicastenango



The Chichicastenango handicraft market is where the recent Mayan populations get together to practice the exchange as their ancesters. The culture, religions, and customs can be clearly visible here. You will have the chance to visit and see the beauty of Atitlan Lake.

The tour starts at 7 am, after to pick you at you hotel, our staff will begin the trip to Chichicastenango village which is at 3 hours from Guatemala City. Then our guide will explain you everthing about the handicraft market which they do on days Thursdays and Sundays

The price per person in USD is 85.00.

Atitlan Lake



The Atitlan Lake is considered as one of the most beautiful in the world. Is surronded by Mayan towns which have a lot of culture. On the lake are visibles Mayan people with their handicraft boats flooding in it.

The tour departs at 7 am starting the trip to Atitlan Lake around 3 hours. It arrives to Panajachel, the town at the border of the lake which the boat trips departs on. You will cross the lake and visit the Mayan population of Santiago Atitlan where the guide will explain you their culture and costums.

The price per person in USD is 85.00.
